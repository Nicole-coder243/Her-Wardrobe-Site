FAQ

What is HER WARDROBE?

HER WARDROBE is a conceptual fashion brand built around bold minimalism and the redefinition of feminine identity. It’s not just about what you wear — it’s about what you say without speaking.

Is HER WARDROBE a real brand?

At this time, HER WARDROBE is a creative concept and visual design project. It's a mock brand developed for portfolio and artistic purposes.

Who is HER?

HER is not one woman.
She is a presence. A mood. A movement.
HER is anyone who chooses to show up boldly, quietly, and with intention.
She doesn’t follow trends — she sets her own rhythm.

Can I buy pieces from HER WARDROBE?

While the collections are visual-only at this stage, HER WARDROBE may evolve into a shoppable concept in the future. Until then, you're welcome to explore and be inspired.

Who created HER WARDROBE?

HER WARDROBE was designed by Makayla Sanders, a creative focused on fashion storytelling, brand identity, and minimalist design.However this is again Mockup website design with the idea of "Her wardrobe".

Can I feature Her Wardrobe or use your designs?

No, sorry. All designs and content are part of a design project and are protected. Images used in this project belong to their respective owners and are used for demonstration purposes only.

Why HER?

Because she walks in and the room shifts.
Because she doesn’t need to speak to be heard.
Because her wardrobe speaks for her.
